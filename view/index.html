Who am I?

My name is Maximilien Nadji, a self-taught Linux administration devoted.
I started to use Linux with Fedora 30 in 2019 during my studies to learn various programming languages (such as C/C++, Python, etc...) and bash scripting.
Previously, I was a DevOps consultant who was interested about automation and software deployment but wasn't interested anymore about software development.
I started to specializing in Linux system administration by following a training course and I felt more in my element.
It convinced me to take a certification and I chose the LFCS certification to target linux systems in the broadest sense which I obtained with success.
I spend my spare time by maintaining my knowledge with the aim of gaining Linux mastery (and of course by updating this website).
